Kataleya

Privacy-First Recovery Support Mobile App

React Native Expo SDK 54 TypeScript SQLite X25519 Encryption TweetNaCl
IN DEVELOPMENT

For Technical Audiences

Kataleya is a React Native mobile application built on Expo SDK 54 featuring a three-vault privacy architecture (Surface/Sanctuary/Fortress), end-to-end encrypted sponsor messaging via X25519/XSalsa20-Poly1305, local ML-powered insights engine with 72% confidence gating, and a circadian-aware UI system. Implements hardware-backed key storage, AES-256 encrypted backups, and zero-knowledge data architecture where health data never leaves the device.

For Everyone Else

Kataleya is a private, safe space for people in recovery – like a digital sanctuary that fits in your pocket. It helps you track your journey, connect with a sponsor when you need support, and find calm through breathing exercises. Everything stays on your phone; no one else can see your personal information, not even us. It's designed to be there when you need it, quiet when you don't, and always respectful of your privacy.

The Missing Piece

Most of the inputs are already built — mood logging, breathing sessions, circadian tracking, streak counting. What's missing is the state engine: a small logic layer that reads those values and outputs a state. No ML. No cloud. Just a function that takes signals and returns one of four states. Then the UI adapts. Quietly. Ambiently. Like the app is feeling it with you.

Three-Vault Privacy

Surface (preferences), Sanctuary (health data in SQLite), Fortress (crypto keys in hardware). Health data never leaves your device.

Circadian Awareness

The app knows dawn from night and shifts its color, energy, and prompts to match your natural rhythm.

Sponsor Channel

End-to-end encrypted connection with a sponsor. Water signals presence. Light signals celebration. Messages no one else can read.
